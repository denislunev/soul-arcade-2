HOW TO PLAY

🎯 SOLO

Type any name and get its vibe. Each scan can trigger a random effect — the rare golden one is special. Collect different vibes to unlock stickers.

⚔️ VS

Enter two names. They battle with rock-paper-scissors — rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper, paper beats rock. Same gesture = a tie.

🔤 GUESS

Guess today's hidden name in 6 tries. The letter count is shown up front, and each guess must be a real name of that exact length (random letters won't be accepted — only genuine names count, like word games). After each guess every letter is colored:

ARight letter, right spot
BIn the name, wrong spot
CNot in the name

An invalid guess (wrong length or not a known name) doesn't use up a try. Solve it in 1 or 2 tries for a golden celebration. A new name drops daily — keep your streak alive. 🔥
