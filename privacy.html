SOUL ARCADE II

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

Soul Arcade II is a small browser game. We built it to respect your privacy. This page explains, in plain language, exactly what data the game touches.

The short version: The game does not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any personal information. It does not show ads or sell data. Your game progress stays on your own device.

1. Data stored on your device

Your game progress — verdicts you've seen, stickers, daily streaks, and stats — is saved locally in your browser's storage (LocalStorage) on your own device. We never receive or see this data. If you clear your browser data or uninstall, it is gone.

2. Anonymous player count

To know roughly how many people play, the game keeps a simple count. To do this:

  • On first launch, your browser generates a random anonymous identifier (a UUID). It is not linked to your name, email, or any personal detail — it is just a random string.
  • Once per day, the game sends this random identifier to our counting service so the same browser isn't counted twice that day.
  • Our server stores only numbers — how many players today and in total. The random identifier is held only in a temporary key that automatically deletes itself within 48 hours. We do not keep a list of identifiers.

The result is a simple tally of "players today / total players," which is shown to everyone inside the game's Stats screen. There is no leaderboard, no profiles, and no tracking of what you do.

3. What we do NOT do

  • No personal data (name, email, phone, address) is requested or collected.
  • No advertising and no third-party ad/tracking networks.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of data.
  • No location tracking.
  • No cookies used for tracking.

4. Sharing results

If you choose to share a result, your device's normal share feature opens with a short text and a link. Sharing is always your choice, and the shared text contains no personal data.

5. Children

The game is friendly and contains no inappropriate content. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Because no personal data is collected, no personal data about children is collected either.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top will change. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via the channel where you found the game (for example, the linked social profile).

Soul Arcade II — pure math, no mysticism, no tracking.
